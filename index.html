Facts you should know about Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs, born on February 24, 1955, in San Francisco, California, was an American entrepreneur and visionary. He co-founded Apple Inc. in 1976 and played a significant role in revolutionizing the personal computer industry with products like the Apple Macintosh. Jobs later introduced iconic devices such as the iPod, iPhone, and iPad, reshaping the music, telecommunications, and computing industries. His focus on design, user experience, and innovation propelled Apple's success. Jobs' relentless pursuit of perfection and his ability to create groundbreaking technologies left a profound impact on the world of technology and beyond,Steve Jobs: Visionary, Apple co-founder, innovation pioneer.
